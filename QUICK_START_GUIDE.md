## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: Why don't I see the changes?
**A:** Clear your browser cache:
- Desktop: Ctrl + Shift + Delete
- Mobile: Browser Settings → Clear Cache
- Or: Open in incognito/private mode

### Q: How do I test on mobile?
**A:** Three ways:
1. Open on your phone
2. Use desktop Chrome DevTools (F12 → Toggle device)
3. Resize browser window smaller

### Q: What if something doesn't work?
**A:** Three-step fix:
1. Clear cache
2. Hard refresh (Ctrl + F5)
3. Wait 5 minutes and try again

### Q: Can I change the banners?
**A:** Yes! Contact support to:
- Add new banners
- Change order
- Update text
- Adjust timing

### Q: Will this affect my SEO?
**A:** No, it improves it!
